In-Client NFTs

Introducing altcoins to NFTs, as of v0.8.8.2 prototype is the first of its kind which now features NFT and smart-contract capabilities. This offers the ability for artists, musicians and animators alike to store, encrypt, protect, sell and share their artwork, all from a unified place. Current compatible formats are JPG/JPEG, PNG, GIF (Animation), OGG VORBIS (Audio) in a square format for initial testing for pictures/animation and up to 5 minute sound files for OGG VORBIS audio. This is a soft limit done only for initial testing. These limits will soon be lifted for full release in future versions. In future releases users will be able to fully interact with this system in a intuitive manner with low network fees and reliable availability.
