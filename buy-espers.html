Buying Espers

Here you can find information about trusted exchanges and how to buy yourself Espers

Where to buy Espers

Be safe when buying Espers, always use one of the exchanges below :

Buy fromExbitron (ESP/USDT) Buy fromExbitron (ESP/DOGE) Buy fromVindax (ESP/ETH) Buy fromBTCpop (ESP/BTC) Join Insane Exchange on Discord!


WARNING! - DO NOT BUY ESPERS FROM YOBIT EXCHANGE

Yobit does not have a live version of Espers listed. They have an ancient unsupported wallet with no connection to the blockchain.
They refuse to upgrade despite many requests from the developer team.

CREATE AN ACCOUNT ON AN APPROVED EXCHANGE

First step is to create an account on one of these exchanges:

Exbitron (ESP/USDT) Exbitron (ESP/DOGE) Vindax (ESP/ETH) BTC pop (ESP/BTC) Join Insane Exchange on Discord!

TRANSFER FUNDS TO THE EXCHANGE

In order to be able to purchase some Espers, you need to deposit funds in to your account. Look at the pair which is listed on the exchange

PLACING AN ORDER

Once you have successfully transferred your funds into the exchange of your choice, you'll be able to place an order to buy some Espers.

STAKE YOUR ESPERS

Did you know that you can Stake your Espers in your wallet to receive 500 ESP per mined block ?!? You can also stake by using Evonodes.com
